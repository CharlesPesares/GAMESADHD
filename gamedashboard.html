How To Play

Choose a game with the gallery, then use these quick controls once you are inside.

Dashboard

Browse: Arrow keys, A/D, mouse wheel, drag, or swipe. Launch: Enter, Space, the centered card, or Play Selected.

Simon Says

Watch the glowing pattern and repeat it with clicks or taps. Reverse mode plays backwards, and Time Attack adds a timer.

Hammer & Shield

P1: A attacks, S parries. P2: L attacks, K parries. Time counters, pin guards, and drop health to zero.

Stacker

Press Space, click, or tap to drop the moving block. Match the stack cleanly to keep width and build combos.

Color Sort

Click or tap a tube to pick the top ball, then place it on a matching color or into an empty tube. Sort every color to clear the level.

Orbit Pop

Aim with the mouse, left click to shoot, right click to swap marbles, and use P, Esc, or Space to pause.

Number Race

2-Player: Play RPS to start. The winner mashes their key (A or L) to fill the board. The loser searches the bouncing balls for the target number to interrupt and swap roles!

Neon Pulse

Tap D, F, J, K or touch the bottom lane buttons exactly as the falling neon notes hit the target line to build your combo.
