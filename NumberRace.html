How to Play

  1. Win Rock, Paper, Scissors to become the filler.
  2. The filler secretly picks one number from the board.
  3. After the countdown, the other player must find that target number.
  4. The filler mashes A or L to fill their lane before the target is found.
  5. Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard to race to 50, 75, or 100 blocks.
  6. First player to fill the selected block target wins the arcade race.
