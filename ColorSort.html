How To Play

Sort the balls until every tube is either empty or filled with one color. Pick up the top ball from one tube, then move it to an empty tube or onto the same color.

Controls

  • Click or tap a tube: select and place a ball.
  • Undo: take back your last move.
  • Restart: reset the current level.
