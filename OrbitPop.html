How To Play

Stop the marble chain before it reaches the center. Fire matching marbles into the chain to make groups of three or more, then use combos to clear space fast.

Controls

  • Mouse / touch: aim the launcher.
  • Left click / tap: shoot a marble.
  • Right click / tap: swap marbles.
  • Power-ups: Bomb (White), Freeze (Cyan), Rewind (Magenta), Splash (Orange), Black Hole (Black).
  • P, Esc, or Space: pause or resume.
